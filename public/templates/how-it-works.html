How It Works


1.
TipperCoin authenticates your identity when you Sign In via Twitter. A unique Bitcoin address is then created for you.

2.
Your account becomes activated when you deposit sufficient Bitcoins (0.0002 BTC).

3.
Now you can send Bitcoins over twitter by tweeting at anyone (they do not need an account to initially receive Bitcoins). For example, "@friend, thanks bro, here's 0.01 BTC #tippercoin"

4.
The TipperCoin Bot, @tippercoin, constantly listens for every tweet containing #tippercoin or @tippercoin. The Bot will then parse the tweet, looking for the recipient and send amount. (See Below)

5.
As soon as the funds are sent, both you and the recipient will receive a confirmation tweet.

6.
An address is automatically created for the recipient if they do not already have one. The recipient will then need to authenticate via twitter within 21 days to receive the Bitcoins, otherwise, the Bitcoins are automatically refunded to the sender.
